## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website places on your device when you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website operators. Cookies can be "first-party" (set directly by this website) or "third-party" (set by external services that appear on our pages).

---

## How We Use Cookies

All Inclusive Vacation and Travel uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

- **Analytics:** To understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.
- **Performance:** To ensure the site loads correctly and features function as intended.
- **Third-party services:** Some features on this site rely on third-party providers that may set their own cookies.

We do **not** use cookies to display personalized advertising, and we do **not** sell your data to advertisers.

## Cookies and Tracking Tools We Use

### Google Tag Manager (GTM)

We use Google Tag Manager to manage and deploy tracking scripts on this site. GTM itself does not collect personal data, but it loads other tracking tools described below. GTM is operated by Google LLC. For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our site. GA4 sets first-party cookies that collect information such as your IP address (anonymized), browser type, pages visited, and time on site. This data is used solely to improve site performance and content. It is not sold or shared for advertising purposes.

To opt out of GA4 tracking, you can install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) or adjust your browser's cookie settings.

### YouTube Video Embeds

Some pages on this site include embedded YouTube videos served by Google LLC. When you view a page with an embedded video, YouTube may set cookies on your device even if you do not play the video. These cookies may be used by Google for advertising and analytics purposes. We have no control over YouTube's cookie behavior.

To manage YouTube cookies, visit [Google's Privacy Controls](https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy).

## How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

**Browser settings:** All modern browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies. Instructions for common browsers:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

**Note:** Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.
