# Accessibility Statement

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-10
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-10

All Inclusive Vacation and Travel is committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We believe that every visitor deserves an inclusive and barrier-free experience on https://allinclusivevacationandtravel.com.

## Our Commitment

We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines. Our goal is to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

## Measures We Take

To make our website accessible, we:

- Use semantic HTML to structure content so it can be understood by screen readers and assistive technology
- Provide alternative text for images where appropriate
- Ensure sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
- Design forms and interactive elements to be keyboard-accessible
- Test the website with modern browsers and assistive technology
- Review and update content regularly to maintain accessibility

## Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some content on the website may not yet be fully accessible. We are aware that accessibility is an ongoing effort and we are continuously working to identify and resolve issues. If you encounter a problem, please let us know.

## Feedback and Assistance

If you have difficulty accessing any part of https://allinclusivevacationandtravel.com, or if you need information or services in an alternative format, please contact us. We will work with you to provide the information, service, or transaction you need through a communication method that works for you.

**Contact for accessibility issues:**

**All Inclusive Vacation and Travel**
Email: [contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com](mailto:contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com)
Phone: Email contact only

When contacting us about an accessibility issue, please include:

- The URL of the page where you experienced the issue
- A description of the problem
- The browser and assistive technology you were using (if applicable)
- Your preferred method of response

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within five business days.

## Formal Complaints

We welcome your feedback and will do our best to address any accessibility concerns promptly. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may file a formal complaint with the appropriate federal or state agency.

## Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing process. As web standards evolve and new assistive technologies become available, we will continue to improve the accessibility of https://allinclusivevacationandtravel.com. This statement will be updated as our website changes.
