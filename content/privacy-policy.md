# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-10
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-10

All Inclusive Vacation and Travel ("we," "us," or "our") operates https://allinclusivevacationandtravel.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

## Who We Are

All Inclusive Vacation and Travel is a all-inclusive vacation and travel information site operated by Bowman Web Services LLC, based in Port Orange, Florida. You can contact us at [contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com](mailto:contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com).

## Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways:

**Information you give us.** When you fill out a contact form, request a quote, call us, or email us, we collect the information you provide — typically your name, phone number, email address, service address, and details about what you need.

**Information collected automatically.** When you visit the Site, we and our service providers automatically collect technical information including your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, pages viewed, time spent, referring website, and general location (city/region). This is collected through cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies.

**Information from phone calls.** When you call us, we collect the information you share during the call and may record the call for quality, training, and record-keeping purposes. See the "Phone Calls and AI Assistants" section below.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request
- Schedule appointments and coordinate service visits
- Send you quotes, invoices, confirmations, and service updates
- Improve our website, services, and customer experience
- Analyze site traffic and user behavior to make the Site more useful
- Prevent fraud, enforce our terms, and comply with legal obligations
- Contact you about your request or related services you may be interested in

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Tracking Technologies We Use

We use the following tools to understand how visitors interact with the Site and to improve our services. Each tool is operated by a third party, and the data those third parties collect is governed by their own privacy policies.

**Google Tag Manager (GTM).** We use Google Tag Manager, a tag management system provided by Google LLC, to deploy and manage tracking tags and scripts on the Site. GTM itself does not collect personal information, but it loads and manages other tracking tools (such as Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and advertising pixels) that do collect data. The tools loaded through GTM are listed individually in this section along with their own disclosures. You can learn more about GTM at [tagmanager.google.com](https://tagmanager.google.com/).

**Google Analytics 4 (GA4).** We use Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC, to understand how visitors use the Site. GA4 uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information such as pages viewed, time on page, referral sources, approximate geographic location, device type, and browser type. This data is aggregated and used to improve the Site. Google may use this data subject to its own privacy terms. You can learn more about how Google collects and processes data at [policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites). You can opt out of GA4 tracking by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

**Microsoft Clarity.** We use Microsoft Clarity, a behavioral analytics tool provided by Microsoft Corporation, to understand how visitors interact with the Site. Clarity collects data through first and third-party cookies and other tracking technologies, including behavioral metrics, heatmaps (showing where visitors click, scroll, and hover), and session replays (recordings of how visitors move through the Site). This data is used for site optimization, fraud and security monitoring, and advertising.

Microsoft Clarity automatically masks sensitive content, including all text entered into form fields and drop-down menus. Personal information you type into contact forms is never recorded. For more information about how Microsoft collects and uses this data, see the [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement).

**AI Assistants and Automated Tools.** All Inclusive Vacation and Travel may use artificial intelligence ("AI") tools and automated systems to help operate the Site and respond to visitors. This may include:

- An AI chat assistant that can answer questions about our services on the Site
- AI-assisted email responses and customer communications
- AI-generated or AI-enhanced content (articles, summaries, descriptions) where noted
- Automated systems that handle contact form submissions and route inquiries to the right person

When you interact with an AI assistant on the Site, you will typically be informed that you are chatting with an AI rather than a human. AI assistants are software systems, not people, and may occasionally provide incomplete or inaccurate information. If you prefer to speak with a human at any time, email us at [contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com](mailto:contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com) and a person will respond.

Conversations with our AI assistants may be logged and reviewed for quality assurance, training, and improvement purposes. We do not sell these conversations to third parties. If you have questions about how AI is used on the Site or want a copy of your conversation history, email us at the address above.

**Mailing Lists and Email Marketing.** All Inclusive Vacation and Travel may offer newsletter signups, promotional email lists, and other email-based communications on the Site. When you sign up for any of our mailing lists — either directly through a signup form or by checking a box when submitting a contact form — you are giving us permission to send you email communications related to our services, promotions, news, and updates.

**What we collect.** When you join a mailing list, we collect your email address and any other information you choose to provide (such as your name). We may also collect technical information about how you interact with our emails, including whether you open them and which links you click, through our email service provider.

**Email service provider.** We use Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), an email marketing platform, to manage our mailing lists and send email campaigns. Your email address and related information are stored on Brevo's servers and are governed by Brevo's privacy policy in addition to this one. You can learn more at [brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy](https://www.brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy/).

**How to unsubscribe.** Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Clicking that link removes you from the mailing list immediately. You can also email us at [contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com](mailto:contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com) with the subject line "Unsubscribe" and we will remove you manually within 5 business days.

**We do not sell your email address.** Your email address is used only to send you communications from All Inclusive Vacation and Travel. We do not sell, rent, or share mailing list data with third parties for their marketing purposes.


## Cookies

The Site uses cookies and similar technologies (pixels, local storage, session storage) to make the Site work, remember your preferences, and collect analytics data. Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how the Site works for you.

## Phone Calls and AI Assistants

When you contact us by email or through the Site's contact forms, we collect the information you provide. We do not currently operate a phone system that records calls or uses AI voice assistants on this Site. Click-to-call buttons on the Site connect you directly to a phone line — call handling, recording, and consent are governed by the call carrier and applicable state law. If our phone handling changes, we will update this Privacy Policy.

## How We Share Your Information

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

- **Service providers.** We share information with vendors who help us operate the Site and our business — hosting providers, analytics tools, email delivery services, CRM platforms, and phone/call handling services. These providers are only allowed to use your information to provide services to us.
- **Legal compliance.** We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our customers or the public.
- **Business transfers.** If Bowman Web Services LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

## Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as we need it to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Analytics data is typically retained for 14–26 months depending on the tool. Call recordings are retained for up to 24 months unless a longer period is required by law.

## Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

- Know what information we collect about you
- Request a copy of your information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of certain uses of your information
- Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). European Union residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). To exercise any of these rights, email us at [contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com](mailto:contact@allinclusivevacationandtravel.com) with the subject line "Privacy Request."
